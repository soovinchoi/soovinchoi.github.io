~About~

This is Soovin Choi, who love visual arts: photography, cinematography and design.
Photographer_Filmmaker_Designer_Model
Soovin is an international student from South Korea to study film.
But after taking black and white photography classes at Monterey Peninsula College, she fell in love with the darkroom and photography.

She finds herself in the darkness under the red safelight at the darkroom.
Focus on portraiture, self-portraiture, alternative processes, and fine art photography based in Monterey, California.


She does alternative processes photography and digital media art which is two centries diffrerence.
She is a time traveler artist.

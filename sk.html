Oh No by soovinchoi on Sketchfab

Title: Rather Be Dead
It is a "chaos" of collage. First figure is 'Hurtle' which has horse head but turtle's body.
A horse is fast and a turtle is slow. A horse lives for the race, but turtle lives for the travel.
It sounds better to enjoy the journey of our lives than to have a race with other competitors.

But this modern society makes overly competitive society.
People are suffering from choosing between the race and journey in our lives. It is difficult to enjoy the view during the race, because if you do, you will lose.
But, if you choose to just enjoy the journey,you will get behind.

This confusing figure, Hurtle is reflecting myself who got confused with all the racings and journeys that I am going through.

However, the person saw Hurtle, and this person felt the same with Hurtle and helpless with himself or herself situation which is anxiety and confusion.

Therefore, he or she decided to do kill himself or herself to escape the confusion, but TreeMax, which is combination between tree object and BayMax object, tried to stop him.
But this person felt so helpless and overwhelmed by seeing TreeMax too because there is not even a tree in this space but robots.
Then, this person says, "I rather be dead."

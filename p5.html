Title: Have You Eaten?
In Korea, asking "have you eaten?" is same as "how are you?"
If you did not have a meal yet, we start worry about you and have a meal together or watch you to eat "something."
Have a meal, not skipping a meal is important in our culture. I can tell this sentence, "Have you eaten?" represents our warm heart and caring about others.
